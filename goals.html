Aim and Goals

    Aim:


      The principal aim of "Psychological Safety, Advancement and Review" (PSAR) is to foster a multidisciplinary discourse on the role, mechanisms, and implications of psychological safety in both societal and organizational contexts. The journal seeks to cultivate an enriched understanding of how psychological safety, as a concept and a practice, can be harnessed to build more resilient, inclusive, and effective systems in our modern world.

    Goals:


      Promotion of Scholarly Inquiry: PSAR aims to serve as an intellectual platform for rigorous academic research on psychological safety. We are dedicated to encouraging explorations across a wide spectrum of themes, including but not limited to the individual, interpersonal, organizational, and societal dimensions of psychological safety.
      Advancement of Theory and Practice: By drawing together research from diverse theoretical perspectives and empirical approaches, the journal aspires to contribute towards the advancement of theories pertaining to psychological safety, as well as translate such theoretical understandings into practical strategies and recommendations for real-world applications.
      Interdisciplinary Dialogue: Recognizing the value of holistic and integrative approaches to understanding psychological safety, PSAR encourages contributions from an array of disciplines, such as psychology, sociology, organizational behavior, management science, and education, among others.
      Policy and Societal Impact: PSAR is committed to informing policy debates and social practices related to psychological safety. Our goal is to provide evidence-based insights that can inspire action towards the creation of more psychologically safe environments in various societal sectors and organizations.
      Global and Diverse Perspectives: The journal aims to capture global and diverse perspectives on psychological safety. We welcome contributions from researchers and practitioners across the globe, believing that such inclusivity can significantly enrich our understanding and application of psychological safety.
      Through these goals, "Psychological Safety, Advancement and Review" endeavors to be a leading voice in shaping the future of psychological safety research and practice, facilitating dialogues that inspire positive transformations in our societies and organizations.
